01 :: THREAT_INTELLIGENCE

THE VULNERABILITY

Current RSA/ECC protocols are vulnerable to Shor's algorithm. Quantum adversaries can intercept 5G handovers and compromise user privacy.

THE DEFENSE

Lattice-based cryptography (ML-KEM) ensures that even with a quantum computer, handover keys remain secure.

THE MISSION

Secure 5G handover signaling without exceeding the strict latency requirements of ultra-reliable low-latency communications (URLLC).
